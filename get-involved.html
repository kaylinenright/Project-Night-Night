Monetary Gifts

Project Night Night relies on the generous donations of individuals and companies to fund this charitable program. Project Night Night is a tax-exempt 501(c)(3) not-for-profit corporation. All donations to Project Night Night are tax deductible to the extent of the law.

Tribute Gifts to Celebrate Special Occasions

Your gift will pay tribute to someone special while helping Project Night Night touch the lives of homeless children. We will send a card of recognition to the honoree. Please include the name and address where it sys "Enter Honoree Name." If it does not appear, please email us the information at Kendra@ProjectNightNight.org

Sponsor a Night Night Package ($25)

You donation of $25 will help to underwrite the cost of a Night Night Package. For each donation of $25, a Night Night Package will be delivered to a homeless child in your honor. Each gift allows Project Night Night to reach out and to serve one more homeless child.

Product Donations

Project Night Night spends thousands of dollar and many hours each year disposing of donations that do not meet our guidelines. Please only donate great quality items which the homeless children we help will be proud to own.

Kids Fundraising & Philanthrophy Zone

As a kid, you're always looking for new ways to be awesome. At Project Night Night, we want to give you the tools to do just that -- and in the process, you will help other kids who could use someone awesome by their side.

With our Be Awesome Program, we'll help you raise money and make a real difference by doing lots of things you already do: using your computer to create cool invitations, talking to your friends and family about things that interest you, and throwing fun parties. The Be Awesome Program is about using all that's good in you to help out someone else.

Every $20 you raise lets us put a Night Night Package into the arms of homeless child. With that money, we will send a tote bag filled with an awesome blanket, book, and stuffed animal to a child living in a shelter.
